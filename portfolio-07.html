GAME DESIGN

Plan-IT: A one button game

A game created at the Game Design Competition.

Project Description & Results.

WHAT

In May 2016, I attended a weekend-long Game Design Camp in a group of 4 at Stratford, Waterloo. The objective was to create a game that is played with only one button.

HOW

We began the design process through brainstorming potential game ideas. Our game was built in Java and the graphics were built in Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop.

THE RESULTS

Presenting our game to a panel of judges, we advanced to the final round and won third place for our game.
