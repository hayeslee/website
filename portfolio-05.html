Everest Event Planning App

An app that would bring people closer
to others during events.

Project Description

WHAT

During many events such as hackathons, company-wide socials and networking events, there's usually the disconnect between the host and the attendees. Between the attendees, it's not always easy to connect with people that havse the similar interests. Overall, it is hard to communicate between all parties of an event. Here's where Everest comes in.

HOW

A project started by four people during EngHacks 2016, an engineering-run hackathon at the University of Waterloo, the project was continually designed and developed for the rest of 2016. The aim of the project was to find a problem that we could try to build something to solve and learn a lot during the process.

RESULTS

At the end of the year, we had created a working iOS prototype for the app. We all learned a lot in the process by using technology we've never had experience with. View the website for the app here.
