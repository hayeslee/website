about me

Trak Record: An Android App for Marathon Runners

During the weekend of March 25-27 2016, I took part in WearHacks, a hackathon held in Downtown Toronto. In a group of six, we participated in the "Cosette Sick Kids Challenge" where we designed and developed a mobile android application for use in a large-scale marathon event with the help of Estimote technology.

Things I learned from this experience:

  • Development of an app based on user requirements and constraints
  • Mobile application development in Android Studios
  • The creation of UI/UX Mockups in Adobe Illustrator

PLAN-IT: A Single Player One Button Game

I attended a game design camp in Stratford, Waterloo last year where the objective was to create a game that was controlled with only one button. In a group of four, we managed to take home 3rd place.

During the first night, we brainstormed and thought of creative ways to implement a game that was both simple to learn and fun to play. Thus, PLAN-IT, a simple planet-building game, was born.
