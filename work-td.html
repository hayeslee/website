TD Labs Accelerator

UI/UX Designer | Jan 2018 to April 2018

Background

For four months, I worked on the TD Labs team in Waterloo, Ontario as part of TD’s Innovation Centre of Excellence. I was fortunate to be able to work on a couple of interesting projects (which may not be disclosed here) as well as design exercises and a personal project given to me by my boss.

Design Exercises

Similar to a workshop, we were given the opportunity to practise our design skills with four sessions in the design team. This was a fantastic way to remind myself of the core-focus when designing, which is empathy and the users.

Double Diamond Design Process

With every project that we worked on, we followed the double diamond design process. First, in the discover phase, we performed broad research on potential users, competitors and current best practices given the client brief. We created personas and customer journey maps to aid in the next step, which was to define the problem. Converging all the research done by all members of the design team, we found insights, themes and more importantly, opportunity areas which were then used to form a problem statement. “How might we ___”
