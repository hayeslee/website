LoyaltyONE Case Competition

A business case competition presented to the executive team at LoyaltyOne.

Project Description

WHAT

A case competition held for all the co-ops at LoyaltyOne put the co-ops in groups of 6 to 7. Over the course of two months, a proposal had to be prepared relating to the idea of bringing CPG companies closer to their consumers. The ideas were presented in a 20 minute presentation to the senior leadership team and two teams advanced to the final round to present in front of the executive leadership team. The idea our group chose was a subscription box service.

HOW

Weekly group meetings were held and I was in charge of the designs for the subscription box. This included the branding for the box, box designs as well as a website design.

RESULTS

After hours and hours of preparation and work, our team managed to win the competition!
